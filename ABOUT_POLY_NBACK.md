### How long should I train?

Research suggests benefits from as little as **15-20 minutes per day**, 4-5 days per week. Quality matters more than quantityâ€”focused, challenging sessions beat long, distracted ones.

Yes. The N-Back paradigm is one of the most studied tasks in cognitive psychology.
